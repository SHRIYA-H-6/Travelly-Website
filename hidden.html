Hidden Gems !

Majuli Island, Assam

Majuli Island, nestled in the Brahmaputra River, offers a serene escape with lush landscapes, ancient satras (monasteries), rich tribal culture, and traditional crafts, making it a cultural and natural haven.

Must Visit In Majuli:

  • Kamalabari Satra
  • Auniati Satra

Gandikota, Andhra Pradesh

Gandikota, often called the “Grand Canyon of India,” is a stunning gorge carved by the Pennar River in Andhra Pradesh. This hidden gem boasts dramatic red sandstone cliffs, ancient architecture, and panoramic views. Ideal for nature lovers and history buffs, Gandikota offers a perfect blend of adventure, serenity, and heritage.

Must Visit In Gandikota:

  • Gandikota Fort
  • Pennar River Gorge Viewpoint

Bhandardara, Maharashtra

Bhandardara is a serene hill station nestled in the Sahyadri range, known for its lush greenery, tranquil lakes, and cascading waterfalls. Ideal for nature lovers and trekkers, it offers a peaceful escape from city life. The cool climate and scenic landscapes make it a perfect weekend retreat in Maharashtra.

Must Visit In Bhandardara:

  • Arthur Lake
  • Randha Falls

Unakoti, Tripura

Unakoti is a mystical heritage site in Tripura, renowned for its massive rock-cut sculptures and stone carvings dating back to the 7th–9th centuries. Steeped in legend and spirituality, it offers a rare glimpse into ancient Shaivite worship and artistry, making it a unique destination for history buffs and culture seekers.

Must Visit In :

  • Unakotiswara Kal Bhairava
  • The Waterfall at Unakoti Hills
